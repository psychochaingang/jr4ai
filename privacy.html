Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Data Collection

JR4.AI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The application runs entirely on your local machine. No telemetry, no usage tracking, no analytics, no "phone home" functionality.

API Keys

Your API keys (DeepSeek, OpenAI, etc.) are stored locally on your machine using Windows DPAPI encryption — the same encryption used by Windows for credential storage. Keys are never sent to any server other than your chosen AI provider.

AI Provider Data

When you send a message to JR4.AI, your message is sent directly to your chosen AI provider (DeepSeek, OpenAI, Ollama, etc.) to generate a response. Those providers handle data according to their own privacy policies. JR4.AI has no control over and no responsibility for how third-party AI providers handle your data.

Local Storage

JR4.AI stores conversation history locally in a ChromaDB vector database on your machine. This data never leaves your PC. You can delete it at any time by removing the memory folder.

Logs

JR4.AI maintains local audit logs of system commands executed through the application. These logs are stored on your machine and are never transmitted externally. You can disable or delete them at any time.

Updates

JR4.AI does not automatically check for or download updates. Updates are delivered manually through the distribution platform (Gumroad).

Third-Party Links

The JR4.AI website may contain links to third-party services (Gumroad, DeepSeek, etc.). This privacy policy does not apply to those services.

Changes

This policy may be updated occasionally. Continued use of JR4.AI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
