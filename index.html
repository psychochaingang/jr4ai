How do I set up the Voicebox AI voice?

Voicebox gives JR4.AI natural voice output. Optional — speech works without it.

Step 1: Download Voicebox from voicebox.ai (free)
Step 2: Install and launch it
Step 3: Restart JR4.AI — auto-detects Voicebox
Step 4: Say "speak this" to test

JR4.AI falls back to built-in voice if Voicebox is off.
