How do I set up the Voicebox AI voice?

JR4.AI uses Voicebox for natural voice output. It's optional — speech works without it.

Step 1: Download Voicebox from voicebox.ai (free)
Step 2: Install and launch it (runs at http://127.0.0.1:17493)
Step 3: Restart JR4.AI — it auto-detects Voicebox and uses it for speech
Step 4: Say "JR, speak this" to test it

JR4.AI falls back to built-in offline voice if Voicebox isn't running.
